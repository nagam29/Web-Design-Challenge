Locations of Cities and Humidity

This chart presents the relationships between the locations of cities (by latitudes) and their average humidity. The data was collected on Jan 5 2017. Cities on the latitudes higher than 0 are those in the northern hemispher, and those on the latitudes lower than 0 are in southern hemispher. In general, humidity of the majority of cities are above 60% regardless of their location. In other words, no clear patterns are observed between the latitudes and humidity.
