Project Summary

Whether financial, political, or social -- data's true power lies in its ability to answer questions definitively. This project focuses on answering a question, "What's the weather like as we approach the equator?" Specifically, this site presents the locations (by latitudes) of cities and their temperature, humidity, cloudiness and wind speed.
