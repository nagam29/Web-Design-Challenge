Locations of Cities and Cloudiness

This chart presents the relationships between the locations of cities (by latitudes) and their cloudiness. The data was collected on Jan 5 2017. Cities on the latitudes higher than 0 are those in the northern hemispher, and those on the latitudes lower than 0 are in southern hemispher. In general, cloudiness of the majority of cities including those close to the equator seem to be somewhat concentrated on either 0 or above 70%. However, this relationship appears to be weak.
