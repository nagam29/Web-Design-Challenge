Locations of Cities and Temperature

This chart presents the relationships between the locations of cities (by latitudes) and their average temperatures. The data was collected on Jan 5 2017. Cities on the latitudes higher than 0 are those in the northern hemispher, and those on the latitudes lower than 0 are in southern hemispher. The cities around equator present the higher temeratures in general. The higher the latitudes are, the lower the temperature is since it's winter in this area. The cities at the lower latitudes in general present higher temperature since it's summer in this area.
